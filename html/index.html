Where people listen to Music

While doing something else

People are apt to get boosted by vibes Music has while they lock in for something. For example, when they address a kind of work we need to concentrate on, they tend to listen to Classics to calm down. According to a statistical data, over 90% of people in the world listen to music while doing their work.

Enjoying the moment

We can add the music to the atmosphere as spice. Again, Music has their own vibr and they ca attach their own vibe to the room and control our feelings and mindset, so for example, when we are in a party, we are apt to put on lively music, so that we can make participants more hyped.

After socializations

As the motto of this website, Music is the vastest hobby in the world I believe, so it is actually normal for us to talk about our favorite music with our friends and strangers. Sometimes we got recommended some music by them. If this happens we are of listening to them to get prepared to the next conversations.
